───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HOW TO SIGN IN
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Launch WeekFit.
2. Tap "Sign In with Email" (below Sign in with Apple).
3. Enter the username and password above, then submit.
4. Wait for the Today screen. Sample data loads automatically for the current calendar day.
5. Demo data stays on-device and is never written to Apple Health.

Do NOT use Sign in with Apple for this review pass — that creates a normal account and expects the device’s own Apple Health data.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT TO REVIEW (Guideline 2.1)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

• Adaptive AI health coaching
  Open the Coach tab. Guidance is generated by WeekFit’s on-device coaching engine from the sample health and nutrition dataset.

• Personalized recovery insights
  On Today, tap the Recovery ring to open Recovery details (recovery score, sleep, HRV, resting heart rate, and insights).

• Recovery-first recommendations
  On Today, review the coach insight card and Up Next / planned training. Open the Plan tab for upcoming sessions (including Recovery Walk). Coach recommendations adapt to the sample recovery and training load.

• Intelligent daily guidance based on health data
  Today combines sample sleep, recovery, activity, and nutrition into the day’s guidance and coaching surfaces.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ALSO AVAILABLE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

• Activity — On Today, tap the Activity ring → open “Morning Walk” (or any completed outdoor session) for metrics, heart-rate chart, and route map.
• Meals — Meals tab with pre-logged meals and drinks for today and recent days.
• Plan — Plan tab with today’s evening workout and the next ~6 days of planned training.
• Profile — Open via the avatar on Today / Coach headers.

Tabs in this build: Today · Coach · Meals · Plan
(There is no separate Insights tab in this submission.)
