# WeekFit Privacy Policy Last updated: June 2026 ## Information We Collect WeekFit may access the following information with your explicit permission: * Apple Health data * Workouts and activity data * Recovery metrics * Nutrition records * Hydration records ## How We Use Information The information is used solely to: * Generate personalized coaching recommendations * Display fitness, recovery, nutrition and hydration insights * Improve the user experience ## Data Storage User data is stored locally on the user's device. WeekFit does not sell, rent, or share personal health data with third parties. ## Apple Health HealthKit data is used only to provide health and fitness functionality. HealthKit data is never used for advertising and is never sold to third parties. ## Contact For privacy-related questions: [mkemiazhuk@gmail.com](mailto:mkemiazhuk@gmail.com)
